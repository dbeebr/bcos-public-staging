# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the BCOS project a respectful and
constructive experience for everyone, regardless of background, experience level,
or identity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respectfully disagreeing on technical or design matters
- Focusing feedback on ideas rather than people
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully
- Prioritizing privacy and safety in all contributions

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or exclusion of any kind
- Publishing private information about others without consent
- Personal attacks or derogatory comments
- Sustained disruption of discussions or contributions

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces: repository issues, pull
requests, discussions, and any other official project communication channel.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a private issue
or contacting the maintainers directly. All reports will be reviewed promptly.
Maintainers will take appropriate action including warnings, temporary bans,
or permanent removal from the project.
