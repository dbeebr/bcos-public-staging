# Genre Notes

*(Fictional example knowledge. Append new genres as new sections — the
set-brief prompt references these headings.)*

## House

- Tempo: 120–128 BPM.
- Sub-styles in our vocabulary: deep house (warm, late-night), classic house
  (vocal, celebratory), afro house (percussive, building).
- Set-brief vocabulary: "rolling", "warm", "hands-up moments".

## Techno

- Tempo: 128–140 BPM.
- Sub-styles in our vocabulary: melodic techno (emotional, arcing), peak-time
  (driving, relentless), dub techno (spacious, hypnotic).
- Set-brief vocabulary: "driving", "hypnotic", "industrial edges".
