# Venue Profiles

*(Fictional venues. Text only — no photos, per HUMAN-GATE-20260630-001.)*

## Harbor Loft

- Capacity ~250, converted warehouse, long narrow room.
- Sound: strong low end; melodic material carries well.
- Best fit: melodic techno, deep house. Peak-time techno overwhelms the room.

## Café Meridian

- Capacity ~80, seated-to-standing, early curfew (23:00).
- Best fit: ambient openers, deep house at low volume.

## The Signal Yard

- Capacity ~600, open-air courtyard, summer bookings only.
- Best fit: afro house daytime, peak-time techno after dark.
